BizSkills For Good

Access changes everything. So we build access.

Our vision

A world where the most powerful tools of an era are not reserved for the people who already have everything. Artificial intelligence is that tool now. We intend to put it, safely, in the hands of communities that would never have had that access.

Where we come from

BizSkills For Good began with underresourced entrepreneurs and communities: practical business skills, coaching founders from idea to company, and supporting ownership through entrepreneurship by acquisition. That work taught us one lesson we have never let go of: talent is everywhere, access is not.

Our partnership strategy

We partner with globally leading AI research labs to close that gap. The exchange is simple and honest. Labs bring frontier tools and safety expertise. We bring trusted community relationships, on-the-ground delivery, and the lived context that keeps AI adoption people-centred rather than extractive. Together we turn research access into real capability: training, deployment support, and feedback that flows back to the labs from communities they would otherwise never hear from.

Who we support

Underresourced entrepreneurs and the organizations that serve them. Newcomer and underrepresented founders. Community organizations doing essential work without technical capacity. Small businesses in overlooked places. If access would change their trajectory, they are who this is for.

If you are a research lab, a funder, or a community organization that shares this vision, we should talk.
